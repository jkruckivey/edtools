Engagement Strategy Comparison

How different fan engagement approaches affect total value for a league with 1M fans

Baseline: Passive viewers generate $10/fan/year through advertising ($10M total). Each engagement strategy builds on this foundation with different cost-to-value tradeoffs.

Strategy Total Value Cost Net Gain ROI
Broadcast-Only $10.0M $0 $0 N/A

Broadcast-Only Baseline

Traditional model: show games, collect ad revenue. No engagement investment.

Tactics: None (passive viewing only)

Digital Engagement Popular

Build app, social presence, and gamification to engage fans across platforms.

Tactics: Mobile App (30% adopt, +$5/fan) + Social Media (50%, +$8/fan) + Gamification (25%, +$12/fan)

Betting-Integrated High Upside

Partner with sportsbooks for betting + fantasy integration. Revenue share model means minimal cost.

Tactics: Betting Partnerships (10% bet, +$30/fan, illustrative) + Fantasy Integration (15%, +$15/fan, illustrative)

Premium Content High Value

Create exclusive behind-the-scenes content and premium app experiences.

Tactics: Exclusive Content (20% subscribe, +$25/fan) + Mobile App (30%, +$5/fan)

Key Takeaway

Betting partnerships have low upfront costs (revenue share model) and can generate strong ROI.

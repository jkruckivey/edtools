Your Role

You're a league executive with 1 million fans generating $10/year each through passive viewing (ads). Your goal: select engagement tactics (apps, fantasy, betting, content) that increase fan value and grow your affordable rights budget. Each tactic has adoption rates, revenue-per-fan, and costs.

Before You Start: What This Model Simplifies
These assumptions make the model explorable but less realistic:
  • Parallel adoption: Each tactic draws from the full fan base independently — in reality, many fans participate in multiple tactics, and this model treats them as separate for clarity
  • No diminishing returns: Adding more tactics always helps — real strategies face saturation and cannibalization
  • Static fan segments: Fans don't evolve — real fans move through lifecycle stages (casual → engaged → superfan → lapsed)
  • Direct monetization: Engagement = revenue — some engagement builds long-term value that's hard to measure

The numbers are illustrative, not forecasts. Company names are used for context; scenarios are hypothetical and do not represent real financial data.

You can adjust all assumptions once inside the simulator.

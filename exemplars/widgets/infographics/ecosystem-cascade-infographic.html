Why Teams "Overpay" for Stars
Conventional analysis only considers direct revenue (tickets + merch = $8M). Ecosystem thinking reveals the cascade: media rights, sponsorships, and playoff multipliers justify the $12.5M salary. The indirect effects ($35.5M) dwarf the direct effects ($9.5M)—but take 2-5 years to fully materialize.

Arenas Are Real Estate Plays
Modern arenas anchor $500M-$1B mixed-use developments. The real estate appreciation ($30M/year) often exceeds sports revenue. Key insight: 10-15% of seats (luxury suites, club) generate 40-50% of ticket revenue—new arenas prioritize premium inventory over raw capacity. Construction takes 3-5 years, but benefits last 20-30 years.

Fastest-Growing Revenue Stream (With Risk)
Betting revenue grew 40% annually (2020-2024). The media synergy is key: live betting increases broadcast viewing time by 18-25%, which networks pay premium for. However, regulatory risk is real—38 US states have legalized, but federal regulation remains uncertain. Early movers secured $5-20M annual deals before market saturation.

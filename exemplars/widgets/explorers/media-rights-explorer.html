Key Insight

Sports are "appointment viewing"—fans watch live because outcomes matter in real-time. This makes sports one of the last reliable sources of mass, engaged audiences in a fragmented media landscape.

Media rights value is driven not just by audience size, but by predictability and advertiser confidence. Broadcasters pay billions for viewers who actually watch the ads.

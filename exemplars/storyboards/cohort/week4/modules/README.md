## Week 4 Learning Outcomes

**WLO 4.1:** Analyze the five athlete revenue streams (endorsements, owned businesses, investments, media/content, licensing)
**WLO 4.2:** Evaluate owned asset strategy vs. endorsement strategy (equity vs. fees)
**WLO 4.3:** Assess women's sports and emerging sports investment opportunities
**WLO 4.4:** Design post-career transition strategies for athletes

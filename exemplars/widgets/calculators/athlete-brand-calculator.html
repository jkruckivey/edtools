Athlete Brand Calculator

Explore how portfolio allocation affects long-term wealth

What This Tool Does
  • Allocates a hypothetical $50M budget across five revenue streams (endorsements, owned businesses, investments, media, licensing)
  • Projects 20-year wealth trajectories based on different growth assumptions
  • Compares your allocation to preset strategies (Serena Williams, LeBron James, Michael Jordan)
Model Simplifications
  • Growth rates are illustrative, not predictive: Owned businesses at 25% annual growth is aggressive — many businesses fail. Investments at 10% assumes consistent market returns.
  • Endorsement decline is modeled, not guaranteed: The -8% post-retirement decline is a pattern, not a rule. Some athletes maintain endorsement value; others lose it faster.
  • No taxes, fees, or costs modeled: Real wealth is reduced by management fees, taxes, and operating costs not captured here.
  • Celebrity portfolios are estimates: Serena, LeBron, and MJ allocations are approximated from public reporting — not verified financial disclosures.

This tool illustrates how allocation choices affect compounding outcomes. It's designed to build intuition about athlete wealth strategies, not to provide financial advice.

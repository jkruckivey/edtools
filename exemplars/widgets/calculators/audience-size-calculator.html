Sports Media Rights Valuation

A framework for understanding what drives the value of sports broadcasting rights

The Valuation Framework
Rights Value ≈ Audience Scale × Inventory × Ad Value × Strategic Premium
Audience Scale: avg viewers × demographic quality
Inventory: number of games per season
Ad Value: CPM (cost per 1,000 viewers)
Strategic Premium: subscription value, bidder competition, scarcity

Key Takeaway

The NFL's dominance comes from a rare alignment of scale, demographic value, and scarcity. Most leagues excel in one or two of these dimensions — few dominate all three. Understanding where a league sits on each factor explains its rights value better than any single metric.
